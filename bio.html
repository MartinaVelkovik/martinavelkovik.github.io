Biography


It all started..


I was born on 16 May, 2001 in Skopje, where I lived for 7 years, but since then I spent my whole childhood in Rosoman.
I went to Primary School-"Pere Toshev" and I attended another primary school for music-"Lazo Micev"-Kavadarci. Music has always been one of my greatest passions. I have won 3 singing competitions, you can see the last performance here!

I discovered another of my passions in Primary School, and that was the love for Math. I was part of every math competition out there.

The biggest discovery was yet to come, in 7th grade I had the first programming course and it was that moment I realised, I have found my dream job..

Since then, I oriented myself towards studies that will lead me to FINKI. I was one of the best students in Gymnasium "Dobri Daskalov" in Kavadarci, PMA.
Now I am a first-year student at FINKI, and I LOVE IT!

Other than my "Professional life", the most important part of my life is my family. My biggest suporters are my parents.

Few things that "scream" Martina are: Iced coffee, huge love for animals, aesteticaly pleasing stuff, 80s music, creativity and a good book.
